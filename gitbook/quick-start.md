# Quick Start

Get your Web3Me profile in under 2 minutes.

## Step 1: Visit web3me.fun

Go to [web3me.fun](https://web3me.fun) and click **Claim Your Profile**.

## Step 2: Sign in with Twitter

We use Privy for authentication. Sign in with your Twitter account — this becomes your Web3Me username and links your Twitter identity to your profile.

## Step 3: Link Your Solana Wallet

In the dashboard, go to **Wallets** and add your Solana trading wallet address. You can link multiple wallets — all PnL is aggregated across them.

## Step 4: Wait for Sync

Web3Me automatically fetches your trading history from the Solana blockchain via Helius. The first sync grabs up to 1,000 recent transactions. Subsequent syncs run hourly and only fetch new trades.

## Step 5: Customize & Share

- Set a custom accent color and banner image
- Pin your best trades to your profile
- Add social links (Twitter, Telegram, YouTube)
- Share your profile URL: `web3me.fun/yourusername`

## What Happens Automatically

Once your wallet is linked:

- **PnL is computed** per token from on-chain swap data
- **Degen Score** is calculated (your AI-assigned trader archetype)
- **Achievements** unlock based on your trading patterns
- **Leaderboard ranking** is updated hourly
- **Trade calendar** heatmap shows your 52-week activity

## Already a Known Trader?

We've pre-populated profiles for 45+ top Solana KOLs. If your Twitter handle matches, just sign in and claim your existing profile — all your data is already there.
