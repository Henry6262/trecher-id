# Reward Vault

The Reward Vault is the on-chain fund that accumulates revenue from $WEB3ME creator fees and distributes it to top-performing traders.

## How It Works

1. Someone buys or sells $WEB3ME
2. The transaction includes a creator fee
3. 69% of that fee is routed to the Reward Vault
4. The vault accumulates until distribution events

## Distribution Events

The vault distributes funds through two mechanisms:

### Trencher Cup (Seasonal)
At the end of each Trencher Cup season, the accumulated vault is distributed to the top 4 finishers:
- Champion: 50%
- Runner-up: 25%
- 3rd place: 15%
- 4th place: 10%

### Weekly Leaderboard (Ongoing)
Between Cup seasons, a portion of the vault is distributed weekly:
- Top 10 traders on the 7-day rolling leaderboard
- Weighted by rank position
- Automatic distribution every Sunday

## Vault Growth

The vault grows proportionally to $WEB3ME trading volume:

| Daily Volume | Daily Vault Inflow (est.) |
|-------------|--------------------------|
| $10K | ~$69 worth of fees |
| $100K | ~$690 |
| $1M | ~$6,900 |
| $10M | ~$69,000 |

These are estimates based on typical creator fee percentages. Actual amounts depend on the specific fee rate set at token creation.

## Transparency

- Vault balance is trackable on-chain
- All distributions are verifiable Solana transactions
- Historical distributions will be listed on the Web3Me leaderboard page
- No team access to vault funds — distribution follows the defined rules

## The Sustainable Model

Traditional crypto rewards models fail because they rely on finite token allocations or VC subsidies. The Reward Vault is different:

- **Revenue-funded** — grows as long as people trade
- **No inflation** — doesn't mint new tokens
- **Self-reinforcing** — bigger prizes attract better traders who attract more volume
- **Transparent** — on-chain and verifiable

The vault can never be "drained" by the team. It exists solely to reward the best traders on the platform.
