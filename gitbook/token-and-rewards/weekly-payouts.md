# Weekly Payouts

Between Trencher Cup seasons, the Reward Vault distributes funds to the top performers on the weekly leaderboard.

## How It Works

Every Sunday, the weekly leaderboard snapshot triggers a distribution:

1. The 7-day rolling leaderboard is finalized
2. Top 10 traders are identified by realized PnL (SOL)
3. A portion of the accumulated vault is distributed
4. Funds are sent directly to each trader's primary linked wallet

## Eligibility

To receive weekly payouts, you must:
- Have an active Web3Me profile
- Have at least one linked and verified Solana wallet
- Be in the top 10 of the 7-day leaderboard at snapshot time
- Have positive realized PnL for the period

## Distribution Weight

Higher-ranked traders receive a larger share:

| Rank | Share |
|------|-------|
| 1st | 25% |
| 2nd | 18% |
| 3rd | 14% |
| 4th | 11% |
| 5th | 9% |
| 6th | 7% |
| 7th | 6% |
| 8th | 5% |

## Leaderboard Periods

The Web3Me leaderboard supports multiple time periods:
- **1 Day** — most recent 24 hours
- **3 Days** — rolling 3-day window
- **7 Days** — rolling week (used for weekly payouts)
- **All Time** — cumulative since profile creation

Weekly payouts are always based on the **7-day period**.

## When Do Payouts Happen?

- **Snapshot:** Every Sunday at 00:00 UTC
- **Distribution:** Within 24 hours of snapshot
- **Notification:** Winners are notified via their Web3Me dashboard

## Consistency Bonus

Traders who appear in the top 10 for multiple consecutive weeks demonstrate consistent skill. Future seasons may introduce streak multipliers for consecutive top-10 appearances.
