# Points & Achievements

Beyond the Trencher Cup and weekly payouts, Web3Me uses the **Ponzinomics SDK** to add engagement layers that keep traders active on the platform.

## Points System

Points are earned through platform engagement and trading activity. They contribute to your overall profile ranking and unlock achievements.

### How to Earn Points
- **Complete your profile** — add bio, avatar, banner, links
- **Link wallets** — each verified wallet earns points
- **Pin trades** — showcase your best plays
- **Daily login** — streak-based daily rewards
- **Trading activity** — points scale with trade volume and consistency
- **Achievements** — each unlocked achievement awards bonus points

## Achievements

Web3Me tracks 12 achievements across 4 rarity tiers:

### Common (50-200 points)
Achievements for basic platform engagement and getting started.

### Rare (200-500 points)
Achievements for consistent trading and platform usage.

### Epic (500-1,000 points)
Achievements for exceptional trading performance and community involvement.

### Legendary (1,000+ points)
The hardest achievements — reserved for elite traders who demonstrate sustained excellence.

### Achievement Display
Unlocked achievements appear in the **Trophy Case** on your profile:
- Rarity-based glow effects (legendary gets golden shadow)
- Lucide icons for each achievement
- Progress tracking for in-progress achievements
- Displayed between links and trade sections on your profile

## Daily Streaks

Log in daily to build your streak and earn escalating rewards:

| Day | Reward |
|-----|--------|
| Day 1 | 50 points |
| Day 2 | 75 points |
| Day 3 | 100 points |
| Day 4 | 125 points |
| Day 5 | 175 points |
| Day 6 | 225 points |
| Day 7 | 250 points + bonus |

Streaks reset if you miss a day. The longer your streak, the more points you accumulate.
