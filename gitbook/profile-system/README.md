# Claiming Your Profile

Your Web3Me profile is your verified trading identity on Solana.

## Pre-Populated Profiles

Web3Me has pre-populated profiles for 45+ known Solana KOLs and top traders. If you're one of them, your profile already exists with:
- Twitter avatar and banner (via fxtwitter)
- Follower count
- Trading history from linked wallets
- Top 3 winning trades auto-pinned
- Degen Score computed

Just sign in with your Twitter account to **claim** it.

## Claiming Flow

1. **Visit your profile** at `web3me.fun/yourusername`
2. You'll see a banner: "This profile was created for you"
3. Click **Claim It** to go to the login page
4. Sign in with Twitter via Privy
5. Your Twitter identity is matched to the existing profile
6. A verified badge appears on your profile

## Creating a New Profile

If you don't have a pre-populated profile:
1. Go to [web3me.fun](https://web3me.fun)
2. Click **Claim Your Profile**
3. Sign in with Twitter
4. Your username becomes your profile URL
5. Link your Solana wallet(s) in the dashboard
6. Wait for the first data sync (usually within an hour)

## What "Verified" Means

The cyan checkmark badge on profiles means:
- The profile owner has signed in with the matching Twitter account
- Their identity is confirmed via Privy authentication
- Their trading data is tied to wallets they control

Unclaimed profiles (pre-populated but not yet claimed) show trading data but don't have the verified badge.

## Dashboard

After claiming, you get access to the dashboard at `web3me.fun/dashboard`:
- **Profile Editor** — bio, display name, accent color, banner
- **Wallets** — link, sync, and manage Solana wallets
- **Trades** — browse your trading history, pin best trades
- **Links** — add social links (Twitter, Telegram, YouTube, etc.)
- **Setup Checklist** — 4-step guide to complete your profile
