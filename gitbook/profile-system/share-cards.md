# Share Cards

Share your trading identity on Twitter with auto-generated visual cards.

## OG Image (Link Preview)

When you share your Web3Me profile URL on Twitter, Discord, or Telegram, an auto-generated Open Graph image appears:

- **Format:** 1200x630 pixels
- **Content:** Avatar, display name, Degen Badge, key stats, top trade pills
- **Generated:** Server-side on each request (always fresh data)
- **No action needed** — just share your `web3me.fun/username` link

## Share Card Page

A dedicated shareable page at `web3me.fun/username/card` with:

- Full stat display with glow effects
- Degen Badge and archetype
- Top trade pills with PnL
- Animated entrance effects (shimmer, glow pulse, pill slide-in)
- Two share buttons:
  - **Share on X** — opens Twitter compose with pre-filled text
  - **Copy Link** — copies the card URL to clipboard

## PNG Export

Download your share card as a PNG image:
- Rendered via html2canvas-pro
- Full resolution for Twitter posting
- Includes all stats and branding
- Can be saved and shared manually

## Automatic Twitter Text

The "Share on X" button generates:
```
Check out @username's trading profile on Web3Me
web3me.fun/username/card
```

## Custom Branding

Share cards respect your profile customization:
- **Accent color** — used for glows and highlights
- **Avatar** — your Twitter profile picture
- The Web3Me logo appears at the bottom for brand recognition
