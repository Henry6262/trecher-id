# What is Web3Me

**Web3Me is the trading profile platform for Solana.** Think Linktree meets Bloomberg Terminal — your on-chain trading performance, verified and displayed as a shareable profile.

Every trade you make on Solana is indexed, aggregated, and turned into your public trading resume. No self-reporting. No fake PnL screenshots. Pure on-chain truth.

## The Problem

Solana traders have no way to prove their track record. CT is full of:
- Fake PnL screenshots
- Cherry-picked winners
- "Trust me bro" trading calls
- No accountability for bad calls

## The Solution

Web3Me creates a verified trading identity for every Solana trader:

- **On-chain PnL** — Every swap indexed via Helius, aggregated per token
- **Real-time portfolio** — Current holdings with USD values
- **Trading history** — PnL chart, trade calendar heatmap, win rate
- **AI-powered insights** — Degen Score classifies your trading style into 8 archetypes
- **Social proof** — Twitter follower count, custom links, shareable cards
- **Gamification** — Points, achievements, daily streaks via Ponzinomics SDK

## The Game Changer: Trencher Cup

Web3Me isn't just a profile page. It's a **competitive trading arena**.

The **Trencher Cup** is a World Cup-style tournament where 32 qualified traders compete in a Champions League bracket format. The prize pool is funded by **69% of all $WEB3ME token creator fees** flowing into the Reward Vault.

More trading volume = bigger prize pool. Every single transaction grows the pot.

## Backed by Real Infrastructure

Unlike competitors scraping Dune Analytics with 15-minute delays, Web3Me is powered by **DevPrint** — a Rust-based real-time pipeline that:

- Monitors Twitter/Telegram in real-time (3-second polling for top KOLs)
- Detects trades via Helius webhooks (sub-second latency)
- Tracks 77,000+ tokens with outcome labeling
- Runs AI analysis on every trade signal

This isn't a weekend project. It's a full-stack trading intelligence platform.
