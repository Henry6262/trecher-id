# Group Stage & Knockout

## Group Stage

32 traders are divided into 8 groups (A through H) with 4 traders each.

### How Groups Work
- Each group runs for a set period (typically 1-2 weeks)
- Traders are ranked within their group by realized PnL during the group stage
- **Top 2 from each group advance** to the knockout rounds
- Bottom 2 are eliminated

### Group Rankings
Within each group, traders are ranked by:
1. PnL (SOL) during the group stage
2. Win Rate (tiebreaker)
3. Trade Count (tiebreaker)
4. Pre-tournament rank (final tiebreaker)

### Visual Display
Groups are displayed as cards on the Web3Me leaderboard page, showing:
- Trader avatar and username
- Current PnL for the group stage
- Qualification status (advancing or eliminated)
- Live updates as trades happen

## Knockout Rounds

16 traders advance from the group stage into a single-elimination bracket.

### Round of 16 (8 matchups)
Group winners face runners-up from other groups in a crossover pattern:
- Group A 1st vs Group B 2nd
- Group C 1st vs Group D 2nd
- Group E 1st vs Group F 2nd
- Group G 1st vs Group H 2nd
- Group B 1st vs Group A 2nd
- Group D 1st vs Group C 2nd
- Group F 1st vs Group E 2nd
- Group H 1st vs Group G 2nd

### Quarterfinals (4 matchups)
Winners of R16 face each other. Higher seed gets bracket advantage.

### Semifinals (2 matchups)
Final four traders compete.

### The Final
The last two traders standing compete head-to-head. The trader with higher realized PnL during the final period wins the Trencher Cup.

## Bracket Display

The full knockout bracket is displayed on Web3Me with:
- SVG connector lines between rounds
- Winner highlighting with glow effects
- Champion banner at the top with golden styling
- Live PnL updates during active matchups
- Responsive layout for mobile viewing

## Timeline

A typical Trencher Cup season:
- **Weeks 1-4:** Qualification period
- **Week 5-6:** Group stage
- **Week 7:** Knockout rounds (R16 + QF)
- **Week 8:** Semifinals + Final
- **After Final:** Prize distribution from Reward Vault
