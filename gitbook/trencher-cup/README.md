# Trencher Cup

The **Trencher Cup** is Web3Me's flagship competition — a World Cup-style trading tournament where the best Solana traders compete for prizes funded by real protocol revenue.

## Why It's Different

Every other "trading competition" in crypto works the same way: a project allocates marketing tokens, traders farm the airdrop, everyone dumps. Zero-sum game, no sustainability.

The Trencher Cup is fundamentally different:

- **Revenue-funded:** 69% of all $WEB3ME creator fees flow into the Reward Vault
- **On-chain verified:** Only real Solana PnL counts — no paper trading, no self-reporting
- **Structured competition:** Champions League format with group stages and knockout rounds
- **Growing prize pool:** More trading volume = bigger vault = bigger prizes

The vault doesn't empty. It grows with every $WEB3ME transaction.

## Format Overview

```
QUALIFICATION PERIOD (4 weeks)
  → Top 32 traders by realized PnL qualify
    → Serpentine seeding into 8 groups of 4
      → GROUP STAGE: Top 2 from each group advance
        → ROUND OF 16: 8 matchups
          → QUARTERFINALS: 4 matchups
            → SEMIFINALS: 2 matchups
              → THE FINAL
                → CHAMPION CROWNED
                  → REWARD VAULT DISTRIBUTES
```

## Key Rules

1. **PnL is king.** Realized Solana PnL (SOL) is the only metric that matters for qualification and advancement.
2. **On-chain only.** All trades must be verifiable on the Solana blockchain through linked Web3Me wallets.
3. **No sandbagging.** You can't hide wallets. All linked wallets contribute to your total PnL.
4. **Live brackets.** The bracket is updated in real-time as trades happen during the competition.

## The Stakes

With 69% of creator fees flowing to the vault, the prize pool scales directly with $WEB3ME adoption. Early seasons may have smaller pools — but as the platform grows, so does the pot.

This creates a powerful flywheel:
- Better traders attracted by bigger prizes
- More traders = more volume = bigger vault
- Bigger vault = more media attention = even more traders

The Trencher Cup isn't just a competition. It's the engine that drives Web3Me's growth.
