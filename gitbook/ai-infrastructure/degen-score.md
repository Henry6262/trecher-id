# Degen Score

Every Web3Me trader gets an AI-computed **Degen Score** — a 0-100 rating that classifies your trading style into one of 8 archetypes.

## How It Works

The Degen Score engine analyzes your on-chain trading history across multiple dimensions:

- Win rate and consistency
- Position sizing patterns
- Trade frequency
- Hold duration
- Risk/reward ratios
- Drawdown recovery behavior

These signals feed into a scoring algorithm that produces both a numeric score and an archetype classification.

## The 8 Archetypes

| Archetype | Icon | What It Means |
|-----------|------|---------------|
| **Crown** | Crown | Consistent winner. High win rate, smart position sizing, steady profits. The king of the leaderboard. |
| **Gem** | Diamond | Diamond hands. Buys early, holds through dips, exits at the top. Patient and profitable. |
| **Scissors** | Scissors | Quick scalper. In and out fast, takes small profits often. High frequency, low hold time. |
| **Zap** | Lightning | High-frequency degen. Trades constantly, takes big risks, sometimes hits massive wins. Pure energy. |
| **Anchor** | Anchor | Conservative trader. Smaller positions, lower risk, consistent but slower gains. The steady hand. |
| **Trending Down** | Chart | Recovering trader. Currently in a drawdown but showing signs of adaptation. Learning from losses. |
| **Activity** | Heartbeat | Volume machine. Trades a lot but PnL is mixed. Active market participant, still finding their edge. |
| **Sprout** | Seedling | New trader. Just getting started, building a track record. The future whale. |

## Score Ranges

- **80-100** — Elite trader. Consistently profitable, strong risk management.
- **60-79** — Solid trader. Good instincts, room for improvement.
- **40-59** — Average. Mixed results, developing strategy.
- **20-39** — Below average. Needs to refine approach.
- **0-19** — Just starting out or heavy losses.

## Where It Appears

The Degen Score is displayed:
- On your **profile header** as a badge with your archetype icon
- In the **leaderboard** next to your ranking
- On your **share card** for Twitter sharing
- In your **OG image** for link previews

## Can I Improve My Score?

Yes. The Degen Score is recalculated on every data refresh (hourly). As your trading improves:
- Higher win rate → score increases
- Better risk management → archetype may change
- Consistent profitability → moves toward Crown/Gem archetypes

The score reflects your actual on-chain performance. There's no way to game it — only way up is to trade better.
