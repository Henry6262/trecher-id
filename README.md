## 🔒 Professional Transparency

Web3Me is built on a foundation of absolute trust and transparency. We have open-sourced this repository so the community can verify exactly how we handle your data.

### Secure & Minimal Identity Linking
When you connect your Twitter to claim your profile, we use **Privy**—an industry-leading secure auth provider.

*   **What we read:** Your public handle, display name, and profile picture to link your on-chain record to your social persona.
*   **What we NEVER do:** We cannot post, like, follow, or DM on your behalf. We never touch your private messages or store your passwords.
*   **Verification:** This codebase is public so you can audit the authentication flow yourself. **Zero trust required.**

## 🛠 High-Performance Stack

Web3Me is engineered for speed and reliability, leveraging the best primitives in the Solana and Web3 ecosystem:

*   **Framework:** Next.js 16 + React 19 (TypeScript)
*   **Identity & Auth:** Privy + Ponzinomics SDK
*   **On-Chain Data:** Helius RPC + Transaction Indexer
*   **Analytics:** Dune Analytics (Deployer Stats)
*   **Infrastructure:** PostgreSQL + Prisma
