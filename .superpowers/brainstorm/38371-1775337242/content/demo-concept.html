What kind of dynamic demo for the Web3Me landing page?

You already have: trader carousel, 3D dome gallery, orbital circles, featured profile preview, leaderboard, reward pool section, activity ticker. What's missing is a live interactive demo that hooks visitors instantly.

A

Live Profile Builder Demo

Visitor types a Solana wallet address → instantly sees a mini Web3Me profile generated in real-time (PnL, degen score, top trades). "This could be YOUR profile." No login required — pure wow factor.

Why this works: Axiom/Kolscan don't let you preview before signup. This is a "try before you buy" moment that converts visitors into users.
B

Trencher Cup Live Bracket

Animated tournament bracket showing real traders competing in real-time. PnL numbers ticking up/down, matchup results updating, champion spotlight with vault counter showing prize pool growing. The World Cup energy, live on the landing page.

Why this works: Creates FOMO and competitive tension. Visitors see real money being won and want in.
C

Reward Vault Live Counter

A big, animated counter showing the vault balance growing in real-time as $WEB3ME trades happen. Every transaction triggers a visual pulse. Shows daily/weekly inflow rate. "69% of every trade goes here." Below it: countdown to next weekly distribution.

Why this works: Makes the reward system tangible. Numbers going up = dopamine. Traders understand money.
D

All Three Combined

Hero section: Live Profile Builder (the hook). Below leaderboard: Trencher Cup bracket (the competition). Reward Pool section: Vault counter (the incentive). Each section has its own dynamic element that tells part of the story.

Why this works: The landing page tells a complete narrative: "See your data → Compete → Win money." Each section reinforces the next.
