What kind of dynamic demo for the Web3Me landing page?

You already have: trader carousel, 3D dome gallery, orbital circles, featured profile preview, leaderboard, reward pool section, activity ticker. What's missing is a live interactive demo that hooks visitors instantly.

A

Live Profile Builder Demo

Visitor types a Solana wallet address → instantly sees a mini Web3Me profile generated in real-time (PnL, degen score, top trades). "This could be YOUR profile." No login required — pure wow factor.

Why: Axiom/Kolscan don't let you preview before signup. "Try before you buy" converts visitors into users.
B

Trencher Cup Live Bracket

Animated tournament bracket with real traders, PnL ticking up/down, matchup results updating live. Champion spotlight with vault counter showing prize pool growing. World Cup energy on the landing page.

Why: Creates FOMO and competitive tension. Real money being won = visitors want in.
C

Reward Vault Live Counter

Big animated counter showing vault balance growing in real-time. Every $WEB3ME trade triggers a visual pulse. Shows daily/weekly inflow. "69% of every trade goes here." Countdown to next distribution.

Why: Numbers going up = dopamine. Makes the reward system tangible. Traders understand money.
D

All Three Combined

Hero: Live Profile Builder (the hook). Below leaderboard: Trencher Cup bracket (the competition). Reward section: Vault counter (the incentive). Each section has its own dynamic element telling part of the story.

Why: Complete narrative: "See your data → Compete → Win money." Each section reinforces the next.
