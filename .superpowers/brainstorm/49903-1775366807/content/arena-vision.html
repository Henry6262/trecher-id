What makes this section alive:

1

PnL Ticking Animation

Active matchups have PnL numbers that tick up/down every few seconds with a gold flash + scale animation. Creates tension — you see the lead changing in real time. Uses a WebSocket or polling from the leaderboard API.

2

Bracket Progression

When a matchup resolves, the winner's card slides into the next round with a green glow trail. The loser fades to 40% opacity. SVG connector lines light up gold as winners advance. The whole bracket tells a story of who's winning.

3

Vault Counter Pulses

The SOL amount at the bottom has a breathing green glow. On each $WEB3ME trade detected, a brief gold flash ripples across the bar and the number ticks up. The "today" counter increments visibly. Makes the vault feel alive.

4

Champion Mystery

The champion slot has a floating crown animation and a "?" avatar with a gold gradient glow. Creates anticipation. When the final resolves, the winner's avatar and name slam into the champion slot with a particle burst.

5

Responsive Bracket

On mobile: collapses to a vertical bracket showing only the current active matchup with swipe to see others. On desktop: full horizontal bracket as shown. The vault bar sticks to the bottom of the section on scroll.

6

Real Trader Data

Uses real avatars (fxtwitter), real usernames, real PnL from the leaderboard API. This isn't a mock — visitors see actual Solana traders competing with actual on-chain performance. That's what makes it hit different.
